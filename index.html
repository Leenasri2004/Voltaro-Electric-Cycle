Frequently
Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our e-bikes and performance.

What is the range of the e-bike on a single charge? +

Voltaro e-bikes offer up to 120 km range on a single charge depending on terrain, rider weight, and riding mode.

How long does it take to fully charge the battery? +

It typically takes 4–6 hours to fully charge the battery using the standard charger provided.

Is the e-bike suitable for all weather conditions? +

Yes, Voltaro bikes are designed to handle light rain and rough terrain, but extreme weather exposure should be avoided.

Can I ride the e-bike without using the motor? +

Absolutely. You can ride it like a regular bicycle without motor assistance anytime.

How do I maintain the battery for longer life? +

Avoid complete discharge regularly, store it in a cool place, and use the original charger for best performance.

What is the warranty on the e-bike and battery? +

Voltaro provides a 2-year warranty on the bike frame and a 1-year warranty on the battery.
